This map shows shipwrecks that sunk before 1500 CE utlizing the Oxford Roman Economy Project Shipwreck database. Only ancient debris assumed to have come from a shipwreck rather than those lost or dumped overboard have been included.

Coordinates should not be taken as accurate at a fine scale as precise locations are not revealed by excavators to prevent pillaging.
Source: Strauss, J. (2013). Shipwrecks Database. Version 1.0.
